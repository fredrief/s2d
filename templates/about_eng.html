About the page

This page lets you convert presentation slides in pdf format to a docx document, suited for taking notes in lectures. The service is aimed towards medical students and other students taking notes on computer.

The program will try to sort out the relevant content of the slides and keep the bullet point structure of the text. It is trained using a number of different presentations, but as the presentation style varies a lot, the result is not always as good. However, it usually saves you a lot of time compared to copy-pasting all the content manually.

The page is written by Fredrik Feyling, and I gladly receive feedback, questions or comments on email: fredrik.feyling@hotmail.com
